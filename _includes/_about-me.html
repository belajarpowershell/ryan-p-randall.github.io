As a graduate student in the humanities, I taught what librarians call information literacy skills while being the instructor for many first-year seminar courses or similar lower-division American Studies and Film/Media courses. I have also been a college writing tutor for undergraduate and graduate students.

I am currently refining my critical information literacy instruction skills, learning more digital humanities techniques, and building a librarianship based on critical praxis.
