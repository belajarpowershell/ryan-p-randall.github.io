The Inquiry Process

"Research" is not finding a hidden cave full of facts.

So what is it?

For one, it's a process!

Let's take five minutes to talk about how you currently research things. What do you do? How do you start? How do you end?

So, what do you do?

Where do you tend to look?

Inquiry Process, Simplified

This is a overview, with questions you might ask in each part.

The Seattle Community Colleges have a great description of the research process.

Parts of the Inquiry Process

  • Essential Question: What do you want to know?
  • Proposal & Plan: How & where will you search?
  • Explore & Research: How is your plan working? How can the experts you know help?
  • Put It All Together: When you have trouble getting your thoughts in order, make an appointment with the Writing Center!
  • Share It All: Turn in what you've made!

The Library can help most with the first three parts.
